School Cafe

School cafe at Sennett follows a unique "scratch cooking concept"

Scratch cooking goes beyond a textbook definition. It's the art involved in creating a dish, building it not just with ingredients but also culture and love. It is the culmination of a creative process using the skills and interests of staff and students to heighten the flavor of dishes or even create a completely new dish.
At MMSD Food & Nutrition, we strive to develop and offer more and more scratch recipes. Our team works hard preparing students' meals using whole, basic ingredients, relying less on convenience or manufactured items, and providing uniquely delicious meals cooked up right here in our own district.
